Limitations
------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Lead cis-eQTL only (GTEx egenes = 1 lead SNP/gene/tissue, aggregated across 13 brain tissues); full eQTL weights (all genic SNPs) NOT used -> simplified TWAS/Multi-SNP MR.
- SNPs within a gene locus are in LD; coloc/TWAS treat them as independent (no LD reference). Real fine-mapping needs 1000G/reference LD.
- Matching is by dbSNP rsID (build-independent); SNPs without rsID or strand-ambiguous alleles were dropped.
- Wald-ratio MR uses the single lead eQTL SNP as instrument (no horizontal-pleiotropy/LD adjustment).
- GTEx eQTL are from mostly non-diseased tissue; AD GWAS is case/control. Cross-tissue cis signal aggregated as a simplification.
- No individual-level / controlled-access data used (MetaBrain/UKB-PPP/ADNI/deCODE excluded per DATA_GOVERNANCE.md).
